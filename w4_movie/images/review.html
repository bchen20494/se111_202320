Review of Puss in Boots: The Last Wish

Brian Chen

February 6th 2023

The moive Puss in Boots: The Last Wish was another great movie of Dream Works who made the Shrek movie series. It had the same interesting adventure from Shrek of finding friends and learning important lessons. This moive was better then the previous works using better technology and a more exciting story.
